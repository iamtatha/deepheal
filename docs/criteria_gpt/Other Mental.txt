**Other Specified Mental Disorder Due to Another Medical Condition (294.8 (F06.8))**

This diagnosis applies when:
*   Symptoms characteristic of a mental disorder are present.
*   These symptoms cause clinically significant distress or impairment in social, occupational, or other important areas of functioning.
*   The symptoms predominate.
*   The disturbance is caused by the physiological effects of another medical condition.
*   The presentation does not meet the full criteria for any specific mental disorder attributable to another medical condition.
*   The clinician chooses to communicate the specific reason why the criteria for a specific disorder attributable to another medical condition are not met (e.g., by specifying the symptomatic manifestation).
